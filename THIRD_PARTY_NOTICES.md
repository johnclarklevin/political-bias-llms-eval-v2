This repository reproduces the source experiment for GPT-5.5, adds no-word-limit and no-system-prompt conditions, scores responses with GPT-5.6 Sol, applies a documented two-party-support screen, and adds response-level data, sensitivity analyses, documentation, and visualizations. The vendored snapshot omits Git metadata and local build artifacts. These modifications are not endorsed by The Washington Post.

The political questions used by the Washington Post repository are attributed there to [ModelSlant](https://modelslant.com/) and the paper [“Measuring Perceived Slant in Large Language Models Through User Evaluations”](https://modelslant.com/paper.pdf).
